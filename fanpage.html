About Justin

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter who rose to fame with his debut single "One Time" in 2009. Known for hits like "Baby," "Sorry," and "Peaches," Bieber has become one of the most influential pop artists of the 21st century. His journey from a YouTube sensation to a global superstar inspires millions worldwide.
