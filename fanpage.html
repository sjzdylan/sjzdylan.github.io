About Justin

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter who rose to global fame as a teen and evolved into one of pop and R&B’s defining voices of the 2010s–2020s. Known for hits like “Sorry,” “Love Yourself,” “Peaches,” and “Ghost,” his catalog blends pop hooks with R&B smoothness and electronic production.

Beyond music, Justin has explored fashion, collaborations, and philanthropy. This page is a fan-made celebration of his artistry and impact—timeless and hype-free. ✨
